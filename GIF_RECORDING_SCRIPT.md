## **Recording Tips:**

1. **Resolution**: 1280x720 or 1920x1080
2. **Frame Rate**: 15-20 FPS (keeps file size small)
3. **Cursor**: Show cursor for clicks
4. **Speed**: Move slowly and deliberately
5. **Pauses**: Pause 1-2 seconds on important screens
6. **File Size**: Keep under 10MB for easy sharing
